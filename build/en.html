About me

How I started cooking macarons?

Once, at the opening of the French store L’Occitane, we were treated to macarons. Never before had I tasted such a deliciously delicate dessert. After that, I began searching for something similar, but unfortunately, it was challenging for me to find where one could buy such cakes. Delicious macarons can only be found in good, expensive coffee shops, and even then, only if they are fresh and properly stored, considering their shelf life is 3-4 days. That's why I decided to learn how to cook them myself!

After dedicating a year to it, transitioning from a gas to an electric oven, exploring many websites, and investing in a lot of ingredients, I found a stable recipe for cooking.

I have already helped over 500 of my students learn how to cook macaron cakes. With me, you will undoubtedly succeed because I will share a little secret in their preparation. With such assistance, everyone can master these capricious desserts, regardless of whether you are a pastry chef or just starting this journey!
