About me

How I started cooking macarons?

Once, at the opening of the French store L’Occitane, we were treated to Macarons. Never before have I tasted such a delicious delicate dessert. After that, I started looking and looking for at least something like that. But unfortunately it was difficult for me to find where one could buy such cakes. Only in good expensive coffee shops can you find delicious macarons, and even then, if they are fresh and properly stored. After all, their shelf life is 3-4 days. That's why I decided to learn how to cook them myself!

After spending a year on it, changing the oven from gas to electric, looking at many sites, spending a lot of products: I found a stable recipe for cooking.

I have already helped more than 100 of my students learn how to cook macarons cakes. With me, you will definitely get macarons, because I will tell you a little secret in their preparation. With such help, everyone will get these capricious desserts. It doesn't matter if you are a pastry chef or just starting this way!
